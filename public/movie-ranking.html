The Best Movies According to Me

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Coraline

This is one of my favourite stop-motion animated films. The dark, whimsical world is so captivating.

Spider-Man Movie Series (Especially Spider-Man: Into the Spider-Verse)

The Spider-Man movies, particularly the animated one, have such unique animation and an incredible storyline that blends different universes.

The Proposal

A delightful romantic comedy. I love the chemistry between the leads and how funny and heartwarming it is.
